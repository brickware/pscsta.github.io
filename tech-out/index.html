Tech Out!

Home » Tech Out!

REGISTER HERE

Information

WHO: Recommended for girls grades 7-12 (activities are targeted for this age group)

WHAT: A day just for girls to hear stories from women in technology careers, participate in hands-on activities, network with peers and mentors, and have fun!

WHERE:UW: Paul G. Allen Center for Computer Science & Engineering, Microsoft Atrium (1st floor)

WHEN: Saturday, March 26, 2016, 10:00am-2:30pm

WHY: To allow girls to explore Technology and Engineering careers.

COST: FREE!

FOOD: Lunch (pizza) will be provided. If you would prefer a different type of food please bring your own lunch.

Sponsored by

          

An example schedule in the past:

10:00 – 10:15 Check-in
10:15 – 10:30 Ice breaker activity
10:30 – 11:15 Engineering activity
11:15 – 12:00 User Experience activity
12:00 – 12:30 Lunch
12:30 – 13:15 Computer Science activity
13:15 – 14:15 Computer Science Activity Booths
14:15 – 14:30 Review and Wrap up!
